② Bring the audience in

Your presentation must include some way the audience participates. Pick at least one:

  • Design a small game tied to your topic.
  • Open with a question — collect answers before you show yours.
  • Stage a mini debate or panel.
  • Run a live vote (opinions, predictions, interpretations).
  • Build a touch / play station around the work.
  • Use a quiz, poll, or guessing round.
  • Invite the audience to add to the piece — collaborate live.
  • Let people interact directly with the work itself.
